2020 Summer Experience

Supporting creative work in the classroom can be challenging, and we want to help! In July 2020, we facilitated a summer professional learning experience for teachers who wanted to learn more about supporting creative work with the Scratch programming language. Thank you for joining us!

Every day, participants received an email with a small, creative programming prompt, designed to help participants explore different computational concepts and debugging strategies in Scratch. While the daily email experience is over, you can still view the prompts and access the supportive online community of teachers and facilitators. Learn more about the experience here. Whether you’re brand new to Scratch or an experienced teacher, we hope you’ll join us in getting unstuck!
